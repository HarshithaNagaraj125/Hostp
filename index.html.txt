                                                           DESIGN THINKING


   
    Design thinking is extremely helping in solving problems. It is an iterarive process that helps resolve user issues
    and redefines problems in alternative strategies and solutions.

   Five phases in design thinking

   1. Empathy - objective is to focus on the users requirements
   2. Define - Identifies the exact problem you should work on
   3. Ideate - Strategize the solutions 
   4. Prototype - Finalize the design
   5. Test - Test the design solution


   Goal of design thinking: Solve complex problems from a human perspective

   Tangible benefits of design thinking
   
   Feasibility check
   Clarity and transparency
   Continous improvements
